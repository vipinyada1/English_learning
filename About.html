About Us

"ELWAI - English Learning"


ELWAI will be a web app for English language learners through which they can learn to pronounce English with more accuracy and proficiency. ELWAI can be used by people who want to learn English and belong from any country and from any cultural background. ELWAI can be used by people from any sector like science, politics, arts, etc. Everyone needs English speaking skills to communicate internationally. ELWAI will help everyone from students to employees to learn English speaking. ELWAI can be used by entrepreneurs, students, employees, teachers, or anyone who wants to speak English fluently.

Our aim is to develop an application that will improve the communication skills of the user. ELWAI will be an AI-powered web app for English language learners through which anyone can learn to speak English more fluently. It will help them in English speaking and make them feel confident about communication skills.

Since English is an International communication language and plays an essential role in our lives as it helps in communication. English is used in politics, science, media, or art. Having a good command of English helps us to have more opportunities in life, first of all, our career. Being able to speak English proficiently, makes us able to work in different countries. So, it is important to learn better English pronunciation to enhance communication skills and to speak English effitively.

ELWAI web app will help users to learn to pronounce English which will help them to know to correct pronunciations of any English word, using this web app users can also learn basic English grammar. ELWAI also contains tests for checking users' accuracy. It can be concluded that the ELWAI will have an impact on increasing students’ pronunciation skills and will help them to learn pronunciation English proficiently. ELWAI, an English Learning Web app using AI, will be a fun and engaging app specially designed to help users to improve their English pronunciation.
